## v1.0.0 — 2026-06-15
- Initial release of the GB VHM Physical Damage Upgrades planner.
- Per-slot table with editable Current Max, VHM Replacement enchant, and VHM Cost.
- Live Avg Damage Increase, Cost per Max, and Avg Cost calculations.
- Cost-per-max color coding (green = cheapest, red = priciest) and "best" tag to prioritize upgrades.
- Totals row for remaining slots, total damage gained, overall cost per max, and total cost.
- Saves your inputs automatically; reset restores Beleren's defaults.
